About Us

Jesus Christ is the son of God. He is co-equal with the father. Jesus lived a sinless human life and offered himself as the perfect sacrifice for the sins of all people by dying on a cross. He arose from the dead after three days to demonstrate His power over sin and death. He ascended to Heaven’s glory and will return again someday to earth to reign as King of Kings, and Lord of Lords (Matt. 1:22, 23; Isa. 9:6; Jn. 1:1-5; 14:10-30; Heb. 4:14,15; 1 Corinth 15:3,4; Rom. 1:3, 4; Acts 1:9-11; 1 Tim. 6:14, 15; Titus 2:13)
